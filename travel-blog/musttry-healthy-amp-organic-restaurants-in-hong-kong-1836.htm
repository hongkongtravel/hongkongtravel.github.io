Must-Try Healthy & Organic Restaurants in Hong Kong

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Hong Kong is a bustling metropolis known for its vibrant food scene, from dim sum to street food. But beyond the traditional flavors, the city has also embraced the global trend of healthy and organic dining. Whether you're a health-conscious traveler or simply looking to balance out your culinary adventures, these must-try organic and healthy restaurants in Hong Kong will leave you feeling nourished and energized.

1. Grassroots Pantry: A Pioneer in Organic Dining

Why It Stands Out

Founded by Peggy Chan, Grassroots Pantry is one of Hong Kong’s first plant-based restaurants focusing on sustainability and organic ingredients. The menu is a creative blend of Asian and Western flavors, all crafted with locally sourced produce.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kale & Quinoa Salad: Packed with superfoods, this dish is both refreshing and nutrient-dense.
  • Mushroom & Truffle Risotto: A creamy, dairy-free delight that satisfies comfort food cravings.
  • Raw Vegan Cheesecake: A guilt-free dessert that’s as delicious as it is healthy.

Location & Vibe

Nestled in Sheung Wan, the restaurant has a cozy, earthy ambiance perfect for a relaxed meal.

2. MANA! Fast Slow Food: Eco-Friendly & Delicious

Why It Stands Out

MANA! is all about fast food done right—organic, sustainable, and packed with flavor. Their motto is "Fast Slow Food," meaning quick service without compromising on quality.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mana Burger: A hearty plant-based burger with a homemade spelt bun.
  • Buddha Bowl: A colorful mix of roasted veggies, grains, and tahini dressing.
  • Raw Chocolate Slab: A rich, dairy-free chocolate treat.

Location & Vibe

Located in Central, MANA! has a casual, eco-chic vibe with outdoor seating—ideal for a quick, healthy bite.

3. Locofama: Organic Comfort Food

Why It Stands Out

Locofama brings organic dining to Sai Ying Pun with a menu that’s both wholesome and indulgent. They prioritize organic, gluten-free, and antibiotic-free ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Organic Chicken Tacos: Made with free-range chicken and homemade tortillas.
  • Sweet Potato Fries: Crispy, baked, and served with a delicious avocado dip.
  • Matcha Chia Pudding: A perfect breakfast or post-workout snack.

Location & Vibe

The rustic, laid-back setting makes it a great spot for brunch or dinner with friends.

4. The Park by Years: A Hidden Gem in Causeway Bay

Why It Stands Out

This hidden gem offers a farm-to-table experience with a focus on organic and seasonal ingredients. The menu changes regularly to ensure freshness.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Avocado Toast with Poached Eggs: A classic done right with organic sourdough.
  • Beetroot Carpaccio: A visually stunning and flavorful starter.
  • Cold Brew Coffee: Made with organic beans for a smooth caffeine kick.

Location & Vibe

Tucked away in Causeway Bay, The Park by Years has a minimalist, Instagram-worthy interior.

5. Nectar: A Vegan Paradise

Why It Stands Out

Nectar is a 100% vegan restaurant that proves plant-based food can be both gourmet and satisfying. Their dishes are free from refined sugars and artificial additives.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jackfruit Tacos: A meat-free alternative that’s surprisingly flavorful.
  • Raw Pad Thai: Zucchini noodles tossed in a tangy almond sauce.
  • Lemon Lavender Cheesecake: A light, fragrant dessert that’s completely raw.

Location & Vibe

Situated in Central, Nectar has a chic, modern aesthetic perfect for a stylish dining experience.

6. Treehouse: Kid-Friendly & Organic

Why It Stands Out

Treehouse is a family-friendly spot offering organic, gluten-free, and dairy-free options. Even picky eaters will find something to love here.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cauliflower Pizza: A gluten-free crust loaded with fresh toppings.
  • Superfood Smoothie Bowl: Packed with acai, granola, and fresh fruit.
  • Organic Chicken Nuggets: A healthier take on a kid favorite.

Location & Vibe

Located in Central, Treehouse has a playful, welcoming atmosphere that’s great for families.

7. OVO Cafe: Plant-Based & Artistic

Why It Stands Out

OVO Cafe combines vegetarian cuisine with an art gallery setting, making it a unique dining experience. Their dishes are crafted with organic and locally sourced ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mushroom Wellington: A savory pastry filled with wild mushrooms.
  • Lotus Root Chips: A crispy, addictive snack.
  • Matcha Tiramisu: A dairy-free twist on the classic dessert.

Location & Vibe

Housed in Wan Chai’s OVOlogue space, the café’s artistic vibe adds to the dining experience.

8. VeggieSF: Retro-Themed Healthy Eats

Why It Stands Out

VeggieSF brings a fun, retro-California vibe to Hong Kong’s healthy dining scene. Their menu is vegetarian with plenty of vegan and gluten-free options.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Veggie Laksa: A spicy, coconut-based noodle soup.
  • Avocado & Egg Sandwich: Served on whole-grain bread.
  • Banana Bread: Moist, sweet, and made with organic flour.

Location & Vibe

The Central location is decked out in vintage Americana, making it a fun spot for photos.

9. Confusion Plant-Based Kitchen: Fusion at Its Best

Why It Stands Out

As the name suggests, Confusion blends different culinary traditions into innovative plant-based dishes. Their ingredients are organic and sustainably sourced.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kimchi Pancakes: A spicy, crispy Korean-inspired dish.
  • Mushroom Ramen: A rich, umami-packed broth with handmade noodles.
  • Mango Sticky Rice: A vegan-friendly version of the Thai classic.

Location & Vibe

Found in Tsim Sha Tsui, the restaurant has a sleek, contemporary design.

10. The Cakery: Healthy Sweets & Treats

Why It Stands Out

For those with a sweet tooth, The Cakery offers guilt-free desserts made with organic, gluten-free, and refined sugar-free ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Avocado Chocolate Mousse: Creamy and decadent without the dairy.
  • Almond Flour Brownies: Rich, fudgy, and gluten-free.
  • Turmeric Latte: A warming, anti-inflammatory drink.

Location & Vibe

With locations in Central and Wan Chai, The Cakery is perfect for a healthy afternoon pick-me-up.

Exploring Hong Kong’s healthy and organic dining scene is a fantastic way to enjoy the city’s culinary diversity while staying mindful of wellness. Whether you're a dedicated vegan or just looking for a nutritious meal, these restaurants offer something for everyone.
