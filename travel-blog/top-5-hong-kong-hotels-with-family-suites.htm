Top 5 Hong Kong Hotels with Family Suites

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Navigating the vibrant, pulsating energy of Hong Kong with a family in tow is an adventure like no other. The city is a dazzling kaleidoscope of towering skyscrapers, bustling markets, tranquil hiking trails, and world-class cuisine. Yet, after a long day of exploring, the last thing any parent wants is to be crammed into a standard hotel room, stepping over suitcases and negotiating bedtime in a single, confined space. The secret to a truly successful and relaxing family vacation in this incredible metropolis lies in your choice of accommodation. Opting for a hotel that offers dedicated family suites is a game-changer. These spacious havens provide separate living and sleeping areas, kid-friendly amenities, and that precious commodity: room to breathe.

We've curated a list of the top five hotels in Hong Kong that go above and beyond with their family suite offerings. These properties understand that traveling with children isn't just about finding a place to sleep; it's about creating a home away from home where every member of the family, from toddlers to teens, can make lasting memories.

1. The Landmark Mandarin Oriental, Central

Urban Oasis with Bespoke Family Service

Tucked away in the heart of Hong Kong's prestigious Central district, The Landmark Mandarin Oriental offers a surprisingly serene escape from the city's frenetic pace. While it exudes an air of sophisticated luxury, it is exceptionally welcoming to families, particularly through its impeccable service and thoughtfully designed suites.

The Landmark Suite is the ideal choice for families seeking space and style. These expansive suites feature a separate living room, allowing parents to relax after the kids have gone to bed in the adjoining bedroom. The interior design is a masterpiece of modern elegance, but it's the little touches that make it family-perfect. The hotel's "Family Fun" package often includes perks like complimentary breakfast for children, a dedicated children's concierge to help plan age-appropriate activities, and kid-sized bathrobes and slippers.

Why Families Love It:

Beyond the suite itself, the hotel boasts a world-class wellness focus. While the parents indulge in the stunning two-level Mo Bar and The Amber restaurant, the little ones are captivated by the hotel's partnership with local experience providers. They can arrange private family dim sum making classes or guided cultural walks through nearby Hong Kong Park and the Zoological and Botanical Gardens. The hotel's location also provides easy access to the Star Ferry for a classic Hong Kong harbour crossing, a surefire hit with children of all ages.

2. The Peninsula Hong Kong, Tsim Sha Tsui

Historic Grandeur Meets Modern Family Comfort

An icon in its own right, The Peninsula Hong Kong offers a legendary experience that can be enjoyed by the entire family. Standing proudly on the Tsim Sha Tsui waterfront, this hotel combines timeless colonial elegance with cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of modern family needs.

For families, the Deluxe Terrace Suites are unparalleled. These suites offer a generous living area, a master bedroom, and a sprawling private terrace with breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour and the Hong Kong Island skyline. The terrace becomes a private playground for children to enjoy some fresh air while parents soak in the vista. The hotel's famous fleet of Rolls-Royce Phantoms can even be booked for family airport transfers or a unique sightseeing tour, making arrival and departure an event in itself.

Why Families Love It:

The Peninsula's commitment to family hospitality is exceptional. Upon arrival, children receive a personalized welcome amenity. The legendary Peninsula Spa offers treatments for teens, while the stunning rooftop pool feels like swimming in the sky. The hotel's location is perfect for exploring the Tsim Sha Tsui promenade, the Avenue of Stars, and the Hong Kong Space Museum, all within a short walk. The afternoon tea in the grand lobby is a quintessential Hong Kong experience that even younger children will find magical.

3. Kerry Hotel Hong Kong, Hung Hom

Contemporary Waterfront Resort in the City

If your family dreams of a resort-style vacation without leaving the city, the Kerry Hotel Hong Kong is your answer. Situated on the Hung Hom waterfront, this hotel feels like a breath of fresh air, with a more relaxed, contemporary vibe and an emphasis on open spaces and stunning harbour views.

The Harbour View Grand Room Suite is a fantastic option. It's essentially a massive, open-plan loft-style space with a comfortable living area, a king-sized bed, and a full-sized dining table. The floor-to-ceiling windows showcase a panoramic harbour view that will keep everyone mesmerized. Many of these suites also feature a large balcony, perfect for a family breakfast. For larger families or those desiring more privacy, interconnecting rooms are readily available.

Why Families Love It:

The Kerry Hotel is a haven for active families. It features an impressive 25-meter outdoor heated pool, a large, modern gym, and a jogging track along the waterfront. The hotel's food and beverage outlets are incredibly family-friendly, with a huge buffet breakfast at Big Bay Cafe and casual, delicious pizzas at the Red Sugar bar, which has one of the best outdoor terraces in Hong Kong. It's also directly connected to a large mall, Whampoa, complete with a cinema and supermarkets, adding immense convenience for parents.

4. Hong Kong Disneyland Hotel

Where Magic is Made for the Whole Family

For many families, a trip to Hong Kong is synonymous with a visit to the magical world of Disney. To fully immerse yourselves in the enchantment, there is no better place to stay than the Hong Kong Disneyland Hotel. This Victorian-style masterpiece is more than just a hotel; it's an integral part of the Disney experience.

The Kingdom Club Suite is the pinnacle of family luxury here. These suites are not just rooms; they are themed adventures, often featuring separate bedrooms for kids and parents. Children's rooms might be adorned with Mickey Mouse and friends, creating an unforgettable sleeping experience. Booking a suite also grants access to the Kingdom Club Lounge, which offers complimentary breakfast, afternoon snacks, and evening beverages—a huge cost-saver and convenience for families.

Why Families Love It:

The hotel is a destination in itself. Between meet-and-greets with Disney characters in the grand lobby, the immersive "Imagineering" activities, the themed hedge maze in the garden, and the stunning indoor swimming pool with a waterslide, there's never a dull moment. The proximity to the park via the complimentary shuttle service allows for easy mid-day breaks, a crucial strategy for managing young children's energy levels during a busy park day. The magic truly lasts from morning until night.

5. The Murray, Hong Kong, a Niccolo Hotel, Central

Architectural Icon with Spacious, Chic Suites

Housed in a revitalized 1960s government building, The Murray is a masterpiece of modern architecture and sustainable design. Its unique history and striking aesthetic are matched by a sophisticated yet surprisingly practical approach to hosting families in the heart of Central.

The Murray Suites are exceptionally spacious, a rarity in Hong Kong. The clever layout includes a large living and dining area that is separate from the bedroom, providing the perfect divide for family life. The floor-to-ceiling windows flood the room with natural light, and the minimalist, chic design ensures a calm and relaxing environment. The hotel's signature arch-shaped windows have become an Instagram favorite for parents and teens alike.

Why Families Love It:

The Murray's location is a key advantage for families who love to explore. It is situated at the edge of Hong Kong Park, a beautiful and expansive green space perfect for kids to run around. It's also just a short walk from the Peak Tram terminal, for a journey up to Victoria Peak. The hotel's rooftop restaurant, Popinjays, offers spectacular views, while the more casual Garden Lounge is perfect for a family meal. The staff are renowned for their anticipatory service and are always ready to assist with child-friendly recommendations, from the best playgrounds to child-proofing your suite.
