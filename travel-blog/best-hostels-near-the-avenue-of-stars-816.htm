Best Hostels Near the Avenue of Stars

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Hong Kong’s Avenue of Stars is a must-visit for travelers who love cinema, culture, and breathtaking harbor views. Located along the Tsim Sha Tsui waterfront, this iconic walkway celebrates Hong Kong’s film industry with handprints of legendary stars like Bruce Lee and Jackie Chan. But let’s face it—staying in Hong Kong can be expensive. Luckily, there are fantastic hostels nearby that offer affordability without sacrificing location or comfort.

Why Stay Near the Avenue of Stars?

Unbeatable Views of Victoria Harbour

The Avenue of Stars sits right on Victoria Harbour, offering one of the best vantage points for the Symphony of Lights show. Staying nearby means you can enjoy the dazzling skyline without fighting crowds.

Proximity to Major Attractions

From Tsim Sha Tsui (TST) to Kowloon Park and the Hong Kong Museum of Art, this area is packed with things to do. Plus, the Star Ferry pier is just a short walk away, making it easy to hop over to Hong Kong Island.

Vibrant Nightlife and Dining

TST is famous for its night markets, rooftop bars, and international cuisine. Whether you’re craving dim sum or craft cocktails, you’ll find it all within walking distance.

Top Budget-Friendly Hostels Near the Avenue of Stars

1. Hop Inn on Carnarvon

Location: Carnarvon Road, Tsim Sha Tsui

Why Stay Here?
- Prime Location: Just a 5-minute walk from the Avenue of Stars.
- Modern Pod-Style Beds: Privacy curtains, USB ports, and reading lights.
- Social Vibe: A rooftop lounge with harbor views.

Nearby Highlights:
- Temple Street Night Market (10-minute walk)
- Kowloon Mosque (3-minute walk)

2. Mei Ho Hostel (YHA)

Location: Sham Shui Po

Why Stay Here?
- Heritage Charm: A converted 1950s public housing estate with a retro vibe.
- Great for Families: Private rooms available.
- Local Experience: Close to Sham Shui Po Market, a hotspot for electronics and street food.

Getting to the Avenue of Stars:
- MTR: 15-minute ride to Tsim Sha Tsui Station.

3. Hong Kong Hostel

Location: Nathan Road, Tsim Sha Tsui

Why Stay Here?
- Super Affordable: One of the cheapest options in TST.
- Central Location: Steps away from the Kowloon Mosque and Chungking Mansions.
- Basic but Clean: No frills, but perfect for backpackers.

Nearby Eats:
- Australian Dairy Company: Famous for scrambled eggs and toast.

4. Urban Pack

Location: Tsim Sha Tsui

Why Stay Here?
- Hipster-Friendly: Industrial décor with a cozy common area.
- Free Walking Tours: Great way to meet fellow travelers.
- Close to MTR: Easy access to the rest of Hong Kong.

Don’t Miss:
- The Peninsula Hotel’s Afternoon Tea (a 5-minute walk).

Tips for Booking Hostels in Hong Kong

Book Early, Especially During Peak Seasons

Hong Kong is a year-round destination, but October-December (cooler weather) and Chinese New Year are especially busy.

Check for Hidden Fees

Some hostels charge extra for towels, lockers, or air conditioning. Always read the fine print.

Look for Hostels with Free Wi-Fi and Breakfast

These small perks can save you money in the long run.

Alternative Budget Accommodations

If hostels aren’t your style, consider:
- Guesthouses in Chungking Mansions (budget-friendly but basic).
- Capsule Hotels for a unique, futuristic experience.

Final Thoughts

Staying near the Avenue of Stars doesn’t have to break the bank. With these hostels, you’ll be in the heart of Hong Kong’s action while keeping your wallet happy. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a couple, or a group of friends, there’s a perfect budget stay waiting for you.

Now pack your bags, grab your camera, and get ready to explore one of Asia’s most dynamic cities—without the luxury price tag!
