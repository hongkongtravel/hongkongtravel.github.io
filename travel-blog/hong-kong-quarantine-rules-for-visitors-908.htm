Hong Kong Quarantine Rules for Visitors

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Hong Kong has long been a top destination for travelers, blending East-meets-West culture, stunning skylines, and world-class cuisine. However, since the pandemic, entry requirements and quarantine rules have been a major consideration for anyone planning a trip. Whether you're a business traveler, a foodie eager to explore dim sum spots, or a hiker ready to tackle Dragon’s Back, understanding Hong Kong’s quarantine policies is essential.

Current Quarantine Guidelines for Travelers

As of now, Hong Kong has relaxed many of its strict quarantine measures, but some restrictions remain in place depending on where you’re coming from and your vaccination status.

Fully Vaccinated Travelers

If you’re fully vaccinated, you’re in luck—Hong Kong has eased restrictions for those with approved vaccines. Here’s what you need to know:

  • No quarantine required for most travelers arriving from low-risk countries.
  • Pre-departure PCR test (within 48 hours before flight) is mandatory.
  • Rapid antigen test (RAT) upon arrival, with results available within hours.
  • Health monitoring for three days, but no strict isolation.

Unvaccinated or Partially Vaccinated Visitors

If you haven’t completed your vaccination course, the rules are stricter:

  • 3-day hotel quarantine may still apply, depending on your origin country.
  • Multiple PCR tests before and after arrival.
  • Restricted movement during the monitoring period.

Popular Tourist Activities Under Quarantine Rules

Even with relaxed measures, some travelers may still face short quarantine periods. Here’s how to make the most of your time in Hong Kong, whether you’re free to explore or need to stay in for a few days.

If You’re Quarantine-Free

  1. Victoria Peak & Sky Terrace 428 – No trip to Hong Kong is complete without this iconic view.
  2. Temple Street Night Market – Dive into local street food and bargain shopping.
  3. Lantau Island & Big Buddha – A must-visit for culture and nature lovers.

If You Have a Short Quarantine Period

  • Order-in Michelin-starred dim sum – Many top restaurants like Tim Ho Wan offer delivery.
  • Virtual tours of Hong Kong’s museums – The M+ and Hong Kong Palace Museum have online exhibits.
  • Stream Hong Kong cinema classics – From Wong Kar-wai to Bruce Lee, there’s plenty to watch.

How Airlines & Hotels Are Adapting

With shifting quarantine rules, airlines and hotels have introduced flexible policies to accommodate travelers.

Flight Flexibility

  • Cathay Pacific & Singapore Airlines offer free date changes if quarantine rules change.
  • Refundable tickets are becoming more common for last-minute adjustments.

Quarantine-Friendly Hotels

Some hotels now offer:
- "Quarantine packages" with meal deliveries and fitness guides.
- Extended stay discounts for travelers needing extra days.

Future Outlook: Will Hong Kong Drop All Quarantine Rules?

While Hong Kong has moved toward reopening, some experts predict further easing in 2024. The government is balancing public health with economic recovery, so travelers should stay updated before booking.

For now, the key takeaway is: Check the latest rules, get vaccinated, and plan flexibly. Hong Kong’s vibrant energy is still waiting—you just need to navigate the entry requirements first.
