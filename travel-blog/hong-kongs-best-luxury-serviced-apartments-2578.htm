Hong Kong’s Best Luxury Serviced Apartments

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog
  3. Blog Details

Hong Kong is a dazzling metropolis where East meets West, offering a unique blend of culture, cuisine, and cosmopolitan energy. For travelers seeking the perfect balance of luxury and home-like comfort, serviced apartments are an unbeatable choice. Unlike traditional hotels, these residences provide spacious living areas, fully equipped kitchens, and personalized services—ideal for both short-term stays and extended visits.

Whether you’re a business traveler, a family on vacation, or a digital nomad exploring Asia’s financial hub, Hong Kong’s luxury serviced apartments elevate your experience. Here’s a curated list of the finest options that combine prime locations, world-class amenities, and impeccable service.

The Peak’s Pinnacle: The Upper House Apartment Suites

Unmatched Views and Serenity

Perched atop Hong Kong Island, The Upper House offers serviced apartments with breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour. Floor-to-ceiling windows frame the city’s skyline, while interiors designed by Andre Fu exude minimalist elegance.

Tailored Services

Guests enjoy 24/7 concierge, in-room dining by Michelin-starred chefs, and access to The Upper House’s exclusive wellness programs. The apartments feature walk-in closets, deep-soaking tubs, and curated art collections—making it a sanctuary above the bustling city.

Central’s Urban Oasis: Four Seasons Private Residences

Prime Location for Explorers

Situated in the heart of Central, Four Seasons Private Residences put you steps away from luxury shopping at IFC Mall, top-tier dining, and the Star Ferry Terminal. The seamless blend of residential comfort and hotel luxury makes it a favorite among high-net-worth travelers.

Lavish Amenities

Residents enjoy access to the Four Seasons’ infinity pool, spa, and private chauffeur services. The apartments boast marble bathrooms, custom furnishings, and smart home technology for a truly bespoke stay.

Kowloon’s Hidden Gem: The Peninsula Residences

Timeless Elegance

Overlooking Victoria Harbour from the Tsim Sha Tsui waterfront, The Peninsula Residences redefine classic luxury. Think chandeliers, handcrafted furniture, and butler service that pays homage to Hong Kong’s colonial heritage.

Exclusive Perks

Guests receive complimentary Rolls-Royce transfers, priority bookings at Michelin-starred restaurants, and curated cultural tours of Kowloon’s hidden alleys and temples.

Wan Chai’s Modern Marvel: The St. Regis Residences

Sophisticated Living

Designed for the discerning traveler, The St. Regis Residences in Wan Chai feature bespoke interiors with curated artwork and state-of-the-art kitchens. The neighborhood’s vibrant nightlife and proximity to the Convention Centre make it ideal for business and leisure.

Signature St. Regis Touches

From the famed Bloody Mary cocktails served in your living room to 24-hour astrology-inspired spa treatments, every detail is tailored to indulgence.

Causeway Bay’s Chic Retreat: The Rosewood Residences

A Home Away from Home

Rosewood’s serviced apartments in Causeway Bay offer residential tranquility amid one of Hong Kong’s busiest districts. Spacious terraces, private gardens, and an in-residence yoga instructor set it apart.

Bespoke Experiences

The property arranges private shopping sprees at nearby luxury boutiques, yacht charters to outlying islands, and even personalized dim-sum cooking classes with master chefs.

The Future of Luxury: Tech-Forward Stays at The Murray Residences

Smart Living

The Murray Residences in Central integrate cutting-edge technology with sustainable design. Voice-controlled rooms, AI concierges, and energy-efficient systems cater to eco-conscious luxury seekers.

Green Luxury

Guests can enjoy rooftop gardens, electric vehicle charging stations, and zero-waste dining options—all while soaking in panoramic city views.

For Families: The Ovolo Southside Residences

Kid-Friendly Luxury

Located in Wong Chuk Hang, Ovolo Southside offers spacious two- and three-bedroom apartments with play areas, babysitting services, and easy access to Ocean Park.

Creative Touches

The property’s vibrant, art-filled spaces and complimentary minibars stocked with local treats make it a hit with families and creative professionals alike.

Long-Term Luxury: The Tai Ping Shan Residences by The Hari

Neighborhood Immersion

Nestled in Sheung Wan, these residences blend into Hong Kong’s artsy, café-lined streets. The design reflects the area’s eclectic vibe, with vintage touches and local artisan collaborations.

Community Focus

Residents enjoy invites to underground gallery openings, private tea ceremonies, and guided heritage walks through Hong Kong’s oldest districts.

The Ultimate in Privacy: The Landmark Mandarin Oriental Residences

Discreet Glamour

Tucked above the Landmark Atrium, these residences offer unparalleled privacy with a dedicated team catering to A-list guests. Think private elevators, soundproofed rooms, and VIP access to the city’s most exclusive events.

Wellness-Centric Design

Each apartment features a personal wellness concierge, in-room fitness equipment, and bespoke nutrition plans from celebrity dietitians.

Hong Kong’s luxury serviced apartments aren’t just places to stay—they’re gateways to experiencing the city like a local while indulging in global standards of opulence. Whether you crave harbor views, high-tech comforts, or cultural immersion, these residences deliver unforgettable stays in one of the world’s most dynamic cities.
